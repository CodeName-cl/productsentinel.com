FAQ

Good questions, calm answers.

How does ProductSentinel know my costs?

It reads the cost-per-item you already keep in Shopify and keeps it in sync. So "below cost" always reflects your real numbers — no separate import needed.

Will it change my prices on its own?

Only if you turn that on. By default it alerts you. When auto-revert is enabled, it restores a price you previously set — it never invents new prices. The merchant sets the rules; the robot just enforces them.

Is this a competitor price monitor?

No. It watches your store, not anyone else's. It's not a competitor monitor, an ERP, or a buyer-facing price-drop alert. It's a safety net for your own prices and stock.

When can I use it?

Right now. ProductSentinel is live on the Shopify App Store — install it and you're protected in minutes.
