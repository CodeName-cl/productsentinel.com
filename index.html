Frequently Asked Questions

How does Product Sentinel know my product costs?

Product Sentinel integrates with your Shopify store and can sync with popular inventory management systems. You can also manually set costs or import them via CSV.

Will it automatically change my prices?

Only if you enable auto-correction. By default, we send alerts and you can choose whether to apply suggested price changes manually or automatically.

What about promotional pricing?

You can set up rules for promotions, clearance sales, and loss-leader products. Product Sentinel is smart enough to understand when below-cost pricing is intentional.

We're currently in beta testing with select merchants. Early access subscribers will be the first to know when we launch publicly.
